Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 1985, BuildCorp has grown from a small local contractor to one of the nation's leading construction companies. With over 35 years of experience, we've completed thousands of projects across the country.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has earned us numerous industry awards and the trust of clients ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To deliver exceptional construction solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and environmental responsibility.

Vision

To be the industry leader in sustainable construction innovation, building the future of our communities one project at a time.
