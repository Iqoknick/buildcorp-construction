Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 1985, R-Mingo Construction and Development Corporation has been a cornerstone of the Philippine construction industry for over three decades. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into a leading construction company known for excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality.

From our headquarters in General Trias, Cavite, we've successfully completed countless residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects across the nation, building not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients and communities.

Our journey reflects the Filipino spirit of hard work, integrity, and vision - qualities that continue to guide us as we build the future of the Philippines, one project at a time.
